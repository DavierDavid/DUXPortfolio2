Trip Verified

Trip Verified is a web application that allows users to consolidate their travel plans all into one itinerary. Trip Verified will provide users with an itinerary with each day's activity. Trip Verified also enables users to browse popular destinations and activities all around the world.

Trip verified was designed for someone who loves traveling and is continuously on the move.



Before

Our initial plan was related to banking and finance. However, we didn't go with it because we thought it would be too complicated and we wouldn't have enough time to complete it. After much discussion, we decided to scrap the idea and come up with a new one instead. We wanted it to be something fun, exciting, and something the general public would use. So we came up with this trip planner.



Beginning

Our team first started by looking for reference. We wanted something accessible and straightforward. We took inspirations from giants of the travel industry like Airbnb and Trip advisor.

After many online calls and debates, we finally decided on a design structure.
We then work on a basic wireframe to bring our ideas into life.



Trouble

After the wireframes were done, we started working on the prototype.
It was a slow start at first because, as we did the prototype, there were many design flaws that we did not anticipate. We had to discuss on how we should fix the flaws. But once we fix all the problems, we were soaring.



Almost there

As we were working on the prototype, we found many "broken" links and a lot of "what if".
We had to call for another meeting and start brainstorming on how we can fix it. Thankfully, this didn't take long.
We also didn't want this project to just be another simple trip planner. So we started to brainstorm fresh and interesting ideas that will bring something new to the table but at the same time, actually useful in the real world.



Complete & Take away

We were slightly behind time because of all the hiccups, but I was pleased with the web application outcome.
I think all the hiccups and bumps we faced as we did the project were beneficial because I learned that communication is essential. I also managed to sharpen my communication skills. I am happy with this team, we worked well together, and although there were many criticisms thrown around regarding the design and such, it was all constructive, and none of it was personal.
