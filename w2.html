Raising Awareness of Diabetes

This website aims to raise awareness and give information regarding diabetes.
This website is for people with family members at home who are at risk and are unaware of ways to deal with diabetes.



Before

When I was choosing a topic to bring awareness to, I was clueless. However, the idea came fast because a handful of my family and friends are at risk of diabetes, so I thought to educate myself regarding diabetes. At the same time, I can share the information that I gathered with them.



Beginning

I started by looking for reference. I browse through the government's websites, specifically the ministry of health. They always have a handful of sites promoting something, so I thought that would be an excellent place to start.
I wanted the website's main point to be the information provided, so I was looking for something simple and straightforward.



Almost there

One of the requirements of the website was that there have to be animations. So as I was working on the site, I always thought of different ways to introduce text and images with different animations. I also found icons and logos that are fun and exciting to give the site more life.



Complete & Take away

I'm happy with the outcome of the website. There were a few problems that came up as I was working on the animations, and I didn't manage to fix it due to time constraints. In the future, I might revisit this assignment and improve it
