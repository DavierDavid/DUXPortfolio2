Npal v2

NPal v2 provides a more personalized experience while using essential e-services. Furthermore, students can access information more conveniently.
For this assignment, I improved the app by giving it a more organized and neater layout, and more features are on the app itself instead of redirecting to the website. Information is displayed much more clearly and is more engaging



Before

The idea came quickly to me because any student who has tried the app at least once will agree that the app provides horrible user experience and interface are not right.



Beginning

Even though I knew what I wanted to change, I still looked for references from other school's apps.
I asked around my friends in polytechnics and local universities to see if they think their school's app is any good.
I found out that many polytechnics don't have apps that are user friendly, the only ones that came close were Singapore polytechnic and Republic polytechnic



Almost there

As I was working on the app, I started applying colors and logos because it was boring. I wanted the app not just to have a functioning and easily accessed features, I want it to be engaging.



Complete & Take away

This assignment was my first time working with InVision, and it was challenging.
It's a program I've never known about, much less used. As I was doing the assignment, I came across numerous problems regarding the program, and I wasn't able to achieve the effects and outcome that I wanted.
However, I'm still happy with the outcome
